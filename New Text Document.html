About Cats

Cats are domestic species of small carnivorous mammals. They are the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and are often called house cats to distinguish them from the wild members of the family.

They are famous for their purring, playful pounces, and independent nature.

Fun Cat Facts!

  • A group of cats is called a **clowder**.
  • Cats spend around **70% of their life sleeping**.
  • They have a unique nose print, much like human fingerprints.
  • The oldest known pet cat existed **4,000 years ago** in Cyprus.
